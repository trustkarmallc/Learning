Course Overview

This comprehensive curriculum guides learners through fundamentals, tactics, middle‑game strategies, endgames, and psychological aspects of competitive chess.

  • Duration: 12 weeks (3 sessions per week)
  • Target: Beginners to advanced players
  • Prerequisite: None

Module Breakdown

Module 1 – Introduction to Chess

Learn board layout, piece movements, and basic rules.

Module 2 – Basic Strategies & Openings

Explore popular openings and development principles.

Module 3 – Tactics & Combinations

Identify pins, forks, skewers and solve tactical puzzles.

Module 4 – Middle Game Strategies

Control the centre, plan attacks, and analyze classic positions.

Module 5 – Endgames

Master fundamental endgames and conversion techniques.

Module 6 – Advanced Strategies & Psychology

High‑level openings, counter‑play, and mental preparation.

Module 7 – Evaluation & Improvement

Weekly quizzes, tournament, and self‑analysis.
